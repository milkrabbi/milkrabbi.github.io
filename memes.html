  • iPhone Touchscreen Criticism (2007)

    The iconic comment from "Jeff" on Engadget's article announcing the iPhone on January 9, 2007, right after Steve Jobs' keynote. It predicted failure but highlighted real issues like smudges and lack of tactile feedback that persist today.

    Full comment:

    Touch screen buttons are a bad idea. Touch screen interface without a stylus is going to be a nightmare. Ever try to use a finger on a touch screen? It doesn't work very well. And good luck trying to type on a touchscreen keyboard with no tactile feedback. That's going to be a real treat. This is going to flop big time.
